The Power of a Private Instance

Public Bot

  • Potential for rate limits or restrictions on certain platforms.
  • Shared resources can lead to occasional lag during peak times.
  • Dependent on the main host's uptime.

Your Private Bot

  • Bypass public limitations for full access to sources like YouTube.
  • Maximum performance and stability running on your own hardware.
  • Complete control over uptime and 100% data privacy.

A 2-Minute Setup

1

Download the Installer

Grab the `Playify_Setup.exe` file from the link on this page. It's a standard, safe Windows installer.

2

Run the Setup

Launch the installer. It will place Playify on your computer and open the configuration wizard automatically.

3

Follow the Guide

The app itself will guide you to get the necessary API keys, with videos and links to make it painless.

4

Invite & Enjoy

Once setup is done, you'll get a unique invite link for your bot. Add it to your server, and you're all set!

Your Questions Answered

Is this really free? What's the catch?

Yep, 100% free. I believe powerful features shouldn't be behind a paywall. The only "cost" is that it runs on your own computer, but the app is very lightweight, so you'll barely notice it.

Do I need a powerful PC to run this?

Not at all. Any regular computer or laptop running Windows 10 or 11 is more than enough. I designed it to be super lightweight.

Does my PC need to be on 24/7?

It does. Since the bot is hosted by *you*, your computer needs to be on for it to appear online in Discord. To make this easy, the installer can set the app to launch automatically when you turn on your computer, so you can just set it and forget it.

Where can I get help if I get stuck?

This is an unsupported fork. Open an issue, I might be able to help. Don't bother the original author or their community (i.e. Discord, Github).
